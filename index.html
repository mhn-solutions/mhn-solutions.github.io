FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and how we work.

How long does it take to start a marketing campaign?

We can start your marketing campaign within 2-3 days of signing an agreement. Our team will conduct an initial consultation, understand your goals, and create a customized strategy tailored to your business needs.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We serve clients worldwide. While we're based in Pakistan and Oman, we work with businesses globally. Our team is experienced in different markets, cultures, and digital landscapes across multiple regions.

What industries do you work with?

We work with diverse industries including e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, healthcare, education, fintech, retail, hospitality, and more. Our flexible approach allows us to serve any industry effectively.

How do payments work?

We offer flexible payment options including monthly subscriptions, project-based pricing, and custom packages. Payments can be made via bank transfer, online payment gateways, or other methods. We'll discuss the best option for your business during our consultation.

Can you guarantee results?

While we can't guarantee specific results due to market variables, we focus on data-driven strategies designed to maximize ROI. Our track record shows a 300% average ROI increase for clients. We use transparent reporting and regular updates to track progress.

Do you provide reporting and analytics?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive monthly reports with detailed analytics, performance metrics, and insights. You'll have access to dashboards showing campaign performance, engagement metrics, conversions, and recommendations for optimization.
